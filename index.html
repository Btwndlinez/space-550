The Room

VIP Table Service

A table held in your name, all night.

Bottle service at Space 550 is built around the table, not the bill. Reserve a section in the Velvet Room, on the Mezzanine, or along the main rail — and it's yours from doors to last call.

All table reservations include expedited entry, a dedicated server, and mixers. Minimums vary by night and table location; the team will confirm specifics by email within 24 hours.
